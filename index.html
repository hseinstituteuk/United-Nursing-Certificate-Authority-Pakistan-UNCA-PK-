About CHANG Authority

Established in 2010

With over a decade of experience in credential verification

100+ Partner Institutions

Our Mission

To maintain the highest standards of academic integrity in Pakistan's healthcare education sector by providing reliable, transparent verification services that protect institutions, employers, and patients.

Core Values

  • • Integrity: Uncompromising commitment to truth and accuracy
  • • Transparency: Clear processes with visible outcomes
  • • Efficiency: Timely verification without bureaucratic delays
  • • Security: Protecting sensitive academic information

Accreditation

Recognized by the Pakistan Nursing Council and the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan as the official verification partner for nursing degrees.
